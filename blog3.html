Prime minister Modi on the importance of Sandwich Panels for construction sector in India

  • ADMIN
  • Jan 1, 2020
  • 12 Comments

Sandwich Panel system, under which 1024 flats will be built in Indore, while pucca houses will be built in 6 more states of the country. This technique was used for a time to build a few selected buildings. At the same time, now this technology is effective in making all types of buildings.

On the very first day of the year 2021, Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the country and presented new schemes. In Indore also, 1024 flats will be built with the help of sandwich panel system.

Sandwich panels have a higher strength-to-weight ratio than solid laminates. As a result, they can withstand comparable loads with less structural material, potentially saving money on materials and shipping.

What is Sandwich Panel System?

Sandwich Panel System is a modern technology of building construction, in which the construction work of the building is completed in a very short time. Beams, columns and wall panels of 1024 flats to be built in Indore will already be ready from the factory, where they will have to be fitted on the ground. With this technique, the work will be done quickly and there will be no need of method.

For this project, the central and state government together are spending Rs 128 crore. Indore Municipal Corporation has decided the land with the help of district administration. 16 towers will be built under the project, whose work has been targeted to be completed in about 18 months. The Prime Minister said that these projects will be made with modern technology and innovative process. In this the construction time will be reduced and more affordable and comfortable houses will be ready for the poor. He hopes that by the year 2022, many poor families of Indore will have houses to live in.

The use of sandwich panel systems has increased in the last 40 years

The use of sandwich panel systems has grown rapidly in the last 40 years, which reflects modern engineering. Earlier this technique was used to build the building of industry and big offices. But after proving its effectiveness over time, the sandwich panel system is also being used in the construction of other buildings.

Mann Ki Baat

Prime Minister Narendra Modi remarked on July 22, 2021, that an effort is being made in the country to guarantee that these projects operate as incubation centres, highlighting various breakthroughs developed by engineers and architects.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi, speaking during the 79th edition of his monthly radio programme "Mann ki Baat," said, “Friends, today an attempt is being made in the country to ensure that these projects work as incubation centres. Through this, our planners, architects, engineers and students will know of new technology and experiment with them too. “

He stated that there was a period when even little Contruction would take years to finish, but that this is no longer the case in India thanks to technological advancements.

“Some time ago we had launched a Global Housing Technology Challenge to invite such innovative companies from all over the world. This is a unique attempt of its kind in the country; hence we gave it the name Light House Projects. For Now, work on Light House Projects is on at a fast pace at six different locations in the country, “ he said.

Prime Minister Modi mentioned that modern technology and innovative methods are used in these Light House Projects. “This reduces the duration of construction. Along with that, the houses that are constructed are more durable, economical and comfortable. “

He further talked about the project at Indore where the Pre-fabricated sandwich Panel system is being used in place of brick–and–mortar walls.
