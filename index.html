We Are Environment Friendly


our Dedication Towards being Environment Friendly

Our strategy is to save the environment, natural resources & increase awareness among the people for upcoming challenges in the future where natural sources will be a big challenge for infrastructure as well for every segment. So, our product "Bhargav Panel" is a direct replacement for traditional constructing walls, which require a lot of natural resources such as water, fertile soil, gas, coal, etc. in manufacturing. It helps us save raw materials in the process and results in minimum wastage in the application.

We have changed the design and functioning of our products to make them environmentally viable

Keeping the environment in mind, we are using less water in manufacturing the product. Unlike other products, the manufacturing process does not need autoclaving or any heat process to increase the strength of the product; it just needs natural curing one week after the process. Moreover, Bhargav Panels require nearly 0% of water usage at the time of installation against traditional brick blocks that need a lot of water in their applications for curing them.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

BHARGAV Panels is a light weight interlocking sandwich panel also known as Insta Walls having two facings of fibre cement sheet with a Cementious core (Cement, Fly-ash, and EPS Balls) incorporating with lightweight aggregates and additives.

Length: 1500-3000 mm
Breadth: 600-610 mm
Thickness: 60mm, 75mm, 90mm & 120mm
Production Capacity: 2, 00,000 SQFT per month

BHARGAV Panels increases speed of assembly/Installation/Erection Of Walls, High quality in finish, high fire rating and the solid masonry. Bhargav Panel Having Good thermal and sound transmission co-efficient makes it an ideal solution for almost all internal and external wall requirements. Foremost advantage, it does not require Plastering, it saves saleable area as well as carpet area in comparison with the traditional Brick and AAC block works.

For Futuristic Infrastructure, Bhargav panel can be used because of light weight property about 3.5 times lighter than tradition brick walls in structural works and the structures can be made lighter due to Bhargav panel usage as walls. Bhargav panel is been designed in such a way that It won’t causes harmful effect to the environment or our ecosystem. Bhargav panel saves natural resources, lighter in structure which increases life of the structure sustainability. It leaves minimum carbon foot prints in complete process, reusable/relocatable and value for money. BHARGAV Panels is been designed with special technique with unique Tongue and Groove Joint, so that it can resist in seismic condition as well as high wind load.

BHARGAV Panels can be used as external or internal walls in conventional buildings, remove area housing, transportable houses, demountable housing, disaster shelters, partition walls, fire ratedwalls, sunscreens and Facades on multi level buildings. Panels may be used for floors and roofs. Panels can also be used for animal Pens, sheds, fences and many other farm uses. 1) Low Cost Houses, Aanganwadi
