Board Details

Fiber Cement Board is mainly made of Portland cement and silicate materials as base and cellulose reinforced. One of the most desirable qualities of fiber cement board is that it is highly durable. Unlike wood siding, fiberboard siding doesn't rot or require frequent repainting. It is fireproof, insect resistant, and perfo rms well in natural disasters. Aside from being low-maintenance, fiber cement boards are also energy efficient and contribute to insulating your home. It's 100% free of asbestos, formaldehyde, benzene, and other harmful substances and with excellent performance like high strength, large size, lightweight, fire and waterproofing, green and environment-friendly, high efficiency, and energy-saving. Therefore, this kind of new material is vigorously developed and recommended by the country.
